Musical Hospice

Our musical hospice program is designed to give joy to seniors by way of performances.
Volunteers are needed to help organize performances and workshops across Jersey and California.
It's a great way for musicians to showcase their work in places that really need it.
You don't need to know music to join us! All we ask for is a desire to help others.
You will get service hours for performing and/or helping us set up our performances!
