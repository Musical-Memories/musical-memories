Welcome to Musical Memories

Providing students an exciting and engaging way to develop their skills in the world of music.

We are a student-run nonprofit organization committed to crafting everlasting musical memories for our local communities.


Our Mission:

  • Visit medical institutes and senior living centers throughout NJ and California
  • Conduct performances, workshops, and demonstrations
  • Spread joy in places that truly need it
